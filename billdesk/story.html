A 15-year young journey

That began with simplifying bill payments for India

Read from the start

2016

BillDesk grows to working with all banks/networks for Electronic Bill payments with a reach of 70 plus billers pan-India base of over 1,000 merchants for online payment gateway and recurring payments. We also launch our new brand identity.

Learn more about BillDesk

2015

Funding from General Atlantic and Temasek + Acquisition of Simpel, enabling small businesses to accept payments through their mobile phones.

2014

Acquisition of Loylty Rewardz.

2012

Introduced NPCI’s NACH platform for recurring payments.

2011

Funding by TA Associates.

2006

Launch of the BillDesk online payment gateway + Round-2 funding by SBI and Clearstone Ventures.

2005

Expanded the banks' network reach to 25 banks for electronic bill payment solutions.

2002

Introduced Universal BillPay platform with Kotak Bank.

2001

Received 1st round of funding from SIDBI and Bank of Baroda. Introduced Bill Presentment & payment solution in India. Started providing recurring payments' service via ECS with Bank of Baroda.

2000

BillDesk starts with the simple idea of enabling anyone in India to pay bills online, easily.
