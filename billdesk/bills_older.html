Dedicated Platform with 70 + Organisations in India

Today, 70 + organisations comprising banks, wallets, etc. offer their customers bill presentment services on their platform. These bill presentment services, powered by BillDesk Biller Network, enable payment of multiple bills from the bank’s platform.


300 + Billers present Bills and get paid conveniently

BillDesk Biller Network (BBN), trusted by 300+ billers is the largest biller network in India. BBN has strong partnerships with providers of services in the domains of utilities, telecom & communication and insurance companies to name a few.

BillDesk biller network has a comprehensive technology stack for enabling bill presentment payment: Universal Billpay, Instapay and Cross Border Billpay Platform.

Universal BillPay

BillDesk UBP is a ready and integrated solution comprising technology and content of 300+ billers from BillDesk Biller Network and 20+ partners from BillDesk Recharge Network.

BillDesk enables banks to offer a bill presentment and payment service to their customers to see and pay all their bills at a single location. It’s Universal Bill Payment Platform is enabled by most banks, wallets and leading payment networks in India.

Key features

  • Consistent experience of access across all channels - internet, mobile, kiosks, walk-ins etc.
  • Provides seamless experience to users.
  • Enable registration and account management through assisted service of banks.
  • Securely and seamlessly integrates with bank/wallet platforms using a single sign-in
  • API-based interactions.
  • Allows customers to setup recurring payment, receive alerts and setup standing instructions.

Instapay

A convenient and easy to setup platform which allows customers to make utility and other bill payments instantly.

BillDesk Instapay incorporates multiple platforms - BillDesk Biller Network, Biller Recharge Network and BillDesk Payment Gateway.

Most Banks and many wallets, organisations in India have enabled BillDesk InstaPay solution to add value to their customers , increase customer engagement and build loyalty.

Cross-Border BillPay

For Transactions beyond borders

Our Cross Border solution eliminates business limitations caused by geographic borders. Securely collect bill payments in multiple currency from international customers.
