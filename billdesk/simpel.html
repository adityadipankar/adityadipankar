Simpel Payments


All Remote Payments. Easy Collections

Start collecting payments instantly through the ‘Simpel’ mobile payment app. Simpel, a unique cashless payment collection method eliminates the messiness of handling cash payments and enables any individual or business entity to conveniently collect payments.

How the Payment flow works

Merchant side flow

Send a payment link to your customer using
Bsimpel-the merchant mobile app
OR the web-based interface
OR the software APIs

Customer side flow

Customer will receive the link and proceed to pay through Simpel, the mobile app

Settlement

Payments settlement is completed in 2 working days (as per RBI guidelines)

Collecting payments was never so ‘Simpel’

Auto payment reminders

Remind customers about payment dues with auto reminder feature via app notifications, SMS or email.

Track your payments

Track the status of payments easily through the B-Simpel merchant mobile app or web interface.

Multiple payment options

Collect payments easily from all customers with the widest range of payment options from credit cards, Online EMI and debit cards to net banking and eWallet services.

Minimal Documentation

Start using the Simpel app instantly with minimal documentation requirement.

Instant activation

Begin collecting payments hassle free with instant service activation.

Easy Registration

Simple 3 step registration process ensures effortless switch from traditional cash handling to a unique and convenient cashless payment module.
